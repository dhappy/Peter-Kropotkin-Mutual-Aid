Bibliographical Note

This Dover edition, first published in 2006, is an unabridged republication of the work originally published in book form by William Heinemann, London, in 1902. The Preface, written by the author, first appeared in a 1914 printing of the work. The present edition also includes an article by Thomas H. Huxley, “The Struggle for Existence in Human Society,” which was first published in the journal The Nineteenth Century, London, in February 1888.
